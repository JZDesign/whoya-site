What WhoYa Is

WhoYa is a game that helps kids memorize phone numbers. That's it. It's not an emergency service. It's not a replacement for a phone book. It's a fun way to practice and it uses some well established science to make it work better than repeatedly/passionately telling your kid to remember your number already.

Side note... if you don't know what a phone book is... imagine a backpack full of paper, and on that paper was every person in your town's name, number, and (sometimes) address. People used to have jobs selling those... not that long ago.

Nothing in this world is free

In the app making business, money can be made several ways. Since we are privacy people, we won't sell your data, heck - we don't even collect it. That means we can reasonably make money with ads or with subscriptions.

I hate ads... You?
